About Me

I was born in Brentwood, Long Island in 81'. Growing up I loved playing basketball, I was usually the tallest and dominated as center. Although I was not good at it, I love baseball. In particular the New York Yankees. I have fond memories of a watching them in the late 90's with my father. Till this day Derek Jeter is still the "Captain" to me. After graduating I moved to Rochester, NY to go to college. I started as a mechanical engineering major, and ended up with a B.S in Criminal Justice. While in college I met my future wife and had our first child. Anthony Jr. After graduation I worked for NY State in a juvenile correctional facility. We then moved back to NYC where I continued to work for NYS. In 2013, we packed up and moved to Houston, Texas. After 5 years working in juvenile probation I decided to start a home remodeling company as my brother and I were begining to get into real estate. After 4 years of doing it full time I knew it was time to change course and find something that suited me better.
