Why did I join Generation?

I tried starting my own website business, but lacking confidence, I was having a hard time getting clients. Seeing that Generation not only teaches JavaScript and Java but also helps with interview skills and works with partner companies for apprenticeships, I thought it would be a great opportunity.

What do I Want to do After the Course?

After graduating Generation, I want to get a fulltime remote job in web development. It doesn't matter whether it is front-end, back-end, or full stack. By working remotely, it will allow my husband and I to move to Costa Rica- A dream we've had for many years.

By having a career in web development, I hope it will also allow us to travel. My dream is to live in a country for one to three months at a time so I can really experience the culture especially since I will be working at the same time.
